SwitchUp Poker - How to Play (Manual Override)

Your objective is to form the strongest possible 5-card poker hand and win credits based on the payout table.

Gameplay Flow - Manual Control

1. Click **DEAL** to get your first 5 cards.

2. **Choice Phase:** The game will glow the best card for a Joker move as a hint.

  • **Option A (Hint):** Tap the glowing card. The best Joker (highlighted on the left) is automatically applied. **Auto Hold** is then performed.
  • **Option B (Manual Override):** Click one of the Joker images on the left. The selected Joker image will glow yellow, and you can now tap ANY valid card to apply that specific Joker.
  • **Option C (Hold):** Tap any non-glowing card or click the **CANCEL HINT** button to perform HOLD.

3. **Hold & Draw:** Select your final HOLDs and click **DRAW**.

4. Your final hand is evaluated for payout.
